Welcome to TAI FARM.

Tai Farm is an excellent destination to enjoy a real experience with the serene beauty of the nature. The countryside feels, natural landscapes, breezes and the late evenings are indeed very soothing. Nothing can be more relaxing than to be here including the driveway that leads to our Farm house. We give you a wholesome experience of a pleasant night/day stay.

About us

With just an hour drive from Mumbai, Tai Farms is located at the foothills of the famous Tandulwadi fort Saphale, Palghar. . The House which is completely made out of Jambha Stone (Chira) has 3 King size AC Master bedrooms, a Large Hall, a Swimming Pool with Kids Section and a Large sit-out area.
